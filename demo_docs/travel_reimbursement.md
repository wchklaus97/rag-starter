Travel Reimbursement Policy

Employees may book economy airfare and standard hotel rooms for approved business travel.
Manager approval is required before booking any trip that exceeds 1,500 USD total cost.

Expense submission rules:
- Submit receipts within 10 calendar days after the trip ends.
- Meals are reimbursed up to 65 USD per day without special approval.
- Ride share is reimbursable for airport trips and customer meetings.
- Alcohol is not reimbursable.

International travel requires both manager approval and finance approval.
Reimbursements are typically paid in the next payroll cycle after approval.
