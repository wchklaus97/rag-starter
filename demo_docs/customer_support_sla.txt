Customer Support SLA

Priority 1 issues are service outages or severe production failures affecting multiple customers.
Priority 1 first response target is 15 minutes, twenty-four hours a day.

Priority 2 issues are major feature failures with a workaround unavailable.
Priority 2 first response target is 1 business hour.

Priority 3 issues are normal defects or product questions.
Priority 3 first response target is 1 business day.

Support must post status updates every 30 minutes for active Priority 1 incidents.
Engineering leadership must be paged immediately for all Priority 1 incidents.
