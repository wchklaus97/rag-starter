Employee Onboarding Guide

New hires are expected to complete account setup on their first business day.
IT sends laptop credentials before 9:00 AM local time.

Required first-week tasks:
- Sign in to email, Slack, and the HR portal.
- Complete security awareness training within 3 business days.
- Enroll in multi-factor authentication before accessing customer systems.
- Review the internal handbook and acknowledge the code of conduct.

Managers should schedule a 30-minute welcome session and a role-specific ramp plan by day 2.
Employees receive access to the analytics workspace only after security training is marked complete.
